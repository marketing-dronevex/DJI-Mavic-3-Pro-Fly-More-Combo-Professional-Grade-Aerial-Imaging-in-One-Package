## Camera System  
### Hasselblad 4/3 CMOS Sensor  
Equipped with a 20MP Hasselblad CMOS sensor, the drone captures rich and vibrant photos with exceptional dynamic range, even in low-light settings. Hasselblad’s Natural Color Solution (HNCS) ensures true-to-life color fidelity, making it ideal for professional-grade imagery.

### Telephoto Lens & Zoom  
The dual camera system features a telephoto lens supporting up to 28x hybrid zoom, enabling smooth and precise framing of distant subjects without sacrificing image quality. This versatility allows for creative compositions ranging from sweeping landscapes to intricate close-ups.

### Video Capabilities  
The drone supports up to 5.1K video recording with HDR and D-Log color profiles, providing filmmakers with deep control during post-production. High bitrate encoding formats ensure maximum detail retention and cinematic quality.

## Flight Performance  
Powered by an intelligent battery, the Mavic 3 Pro achieves up to 46 minutes of flight time per charge. It reaches speeds up to 19 m/s and operates effectively at altitudes up to 6000 meters. The enhanced DJI O3 transmission system allows stable control and real-time video feed up to 15 km, even in environments with wireless interference.

## Safety & Reliability  
The drone is equipped with omnidirectional obstacle avoidance sensors that scan in all directions, preventing collisions and enhancing pilot confidence. Advanced GPS and vision positioning systems provide precise hovering and automatic Return to Home (RTH) functions in cases of low battery or signal loss, reinforcing safe operations across different environments.

## Control & Connectivity  
Compatible with the DJI RC Pro and standard DJI RC controllers, the Mavic 3 Pro offers intuitive flight controls, customizable settings, and seamless integration with the DJI Fly app. Real-time telemetry data and crystal-clear HD video provide professional pilots with precise situational awareness during operations.
